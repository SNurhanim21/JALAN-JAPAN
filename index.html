FAQ & Business FAQ

What Days is JJJ Open?

We opens daily, including all public holidays. Any days we are closed, notices will be displayed at our stores and announcement will be also be made via JJJ Facebook page.

Payment Accepted?

Our stores accepts CASH and payment via Credit Card

Does JJJ Entertain Online Purchasing?

We do not have online purchasing facilities. You can only purchase our goods in JJJ stores.

Delivery Approach?

We DO NOT make deliveries. Customers are required to arrange for their own transportation if they are planning to purchase bulky items such as furnitures, etc.

We Only Sell; We Don't Buy Policy
